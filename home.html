Know me better

Nick Castillo

Graduating with a degree in Computer Engineering, Nick sought out a career in technology but was not sure exactly quite where he fit. Nick has a passion for code development, marketing, and community management, which puts him in quite a unique category.

Nick's first calling happened when he transferred departments within Brivo. He started out his career there as a Java developer and eventually moved to their research and development department, Brivo Labs. It was there he assisted in the development and marketing of Brivo Lab's innovative products such as Randivoo Mobile, which allowed and managed users to unlock doors with their phones. The majority of these projects were developed using the crowdsourcing platform, Topcoder, which Nick used in order to manage and execute multiple projects at one time. He was amazed of the intellect and speed of this community and saw how effective this could be for businesses around the world, which was one of the many reasons he knew he needed to be a part of this adventure.

In 2015, Nick was hired by Topcoder as their Developer Evangelist and a year later found himself managing the 1 million + members in this community. He currently co-owns the social media marketing to the Topcoder Community, writes weekly newsletters to the development members, oversees the Community Advisory Board, creates videos as a tool for learning and updating the community, and much more. Each year he gets to travel to a few countries in order to host the Topcoder Open Regional Events and in 2016 was fortunate enough to host the Topcoder Open Finals in Washington DC!
